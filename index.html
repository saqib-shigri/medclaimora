FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What is Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)?

RCM is the process of managing claims, payment, and revenue generation. It covers everything from patient registration to final payment collection.

How long does credentialing take?

Credentialing typically takes 60-120 days. With MedClaimora, we expedite the process through our established relationships with major payers.

What makes your 97.8% clean claim rate possible?

Our certified coders, automated claim scrubbing, and pre-submission validation ensure claims are error-free before reaching payers.

Is my patient data secure?

Absolutely. We are fully HIPAA compliant with bank-grade encryption and strict access controls.
